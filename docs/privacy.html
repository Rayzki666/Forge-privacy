Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: Forge does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Your AI conversations, journal entries, quiz answers, and progress all stay entirely on your device. No account is required to use the app.

1. Who We Are

Forge is a personal development application developed and published by Ray Y ("we", "us", or "our"), available on the Google Play Store under the package name com.human30.mapper.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your AI coaching conversations
  • Your journal entries
  • Your quiz answers or personal assessment scores
  • Your visions, goals, or any reflections you write
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers or advertising IDs

3. On-Device AI — How It Works

The AI coach in Forge runs a large language model (LLM) downloaded directly to your phone. All inference — every question you ask and every response the AI generates — happens locally on your device. No conversation data is ever sent to any server, including ours.

The model file is downloaded once from a public repository (Hugging Face) over a standard HTTPS connection and stored in your device's private application directory. After download, the AI works with no internet connection whatsoever.

4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Forge stores the following data locally using Android's private app storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app:

  • Your quiz answers and quadrant scores
  • Journal entries and channel reflections you write
  • AI coaching conversation history
  • App progress (XP, streak, completed channels, programs)
  • Vision statements and goals you enter
  • Subscription status (stored locally, verified via the payment processor)

5. Subscription & Payment

Forge offers an optional subscription through the Google Play Store, processed by Google. We do not handle or store any payment information. Purchase receipts and subscription status are managed entirely by Google Play and Superwall (our paywall infrastructure provider). Please review Google's Privacy Policy and Superwall's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

Superwall may receive anonymised, non-identifying signals related to subscription events (e.g. "paywall shown", "purchase completed") to facilitate billing. No personal data or app content is shared.

6. Permissions We Request

  • Internet access — required to download the AI model file on first setup and to process subscription payments via Google Play. Not used during AI conversations.
  • Notifications — optional, used only to alert you when a timed channel practice ends. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Forge is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18. If you believe a minor is using the app, please contact us so we can assist.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app.
